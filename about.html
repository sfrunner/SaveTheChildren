Documentation

How environmentally safe is the city you live in or planning your trip to? airGeek is a web application that allows you to input a location and receive information regarding its air current pollution, UV index, and air quality. This application allows users to see and compare the environmentally best locations to live, or just visit. It will utilize the framework Angular.js and four APIâ€™s: Google Maps Geocoding, Google place API, AQI, and Open Weather (UV Index, Weather). Once a user has searched a location, they have the option to store the information in a table so that they can seamlessly compare the livability of multiple locations. The goal of this site is to provide, display, and record data regarding Air pollution and Quality in a seamless, visually pleasing and user friendly way.
